About Me

Hey! I'm Prem Acharya, a Grade XII student from Nepal, currently diving deep into the Science stream. Science, especially Physics and Mathematics, has always been my playground, where curiosity and passion drive me to explore beyond the textbooks. I’m actively involved in research on cutting-edge topics like quantum processors, PEM fuel cells, and bacteria fuel cells. As the coordinator of the Kanti Science Club, I organize events like speech competitions, science exhibitions, and Children’s Day programs, collaborating with notable personalities in our district to foster a love for science among students. My projects are diverse, from nanotechnology applications in daily life to studying the mechanisms of engines at local automobile workshops. Beyond academics, I’m an avid chess enthusiast, part of the Kanti Chess Club, where I sharpen my mental skills and challenge myself with strategies and tactics. My goal is to further my education in the United States, with a dream to secure a scholarship to best University, where I hope to continue my journey of academic exploration and discovery. I also enjoy writing poetry, delving into metaphors and emotional depth, and curating a website for students and research enthusiasts, where I share insights into Physics, Mathematics, and beyond. Life’s a journey, and I’m here to make every step a leap towards knowledge and growth!
